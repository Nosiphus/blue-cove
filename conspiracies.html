Conspiracies with Nate Sawyers - Blue Cove

Conspiracies with Nate Sawyers was the last series to be recorded in 2015 and has long been considered to be one of the best series ever recorded on the Nosiphus channel. In the series, Nate Sawyers dons the identity of a conspiracy theorist reporting from deep within an underground bunker.
