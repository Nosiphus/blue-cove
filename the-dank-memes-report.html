The Dank Memes Report was recorded in July 2016 after Wyatt Mote wanted to release videos in a similar fashion to Nate Sawyers' Conspiracies with Nate in December 2015. Two videos were released and three more were recorded. This series has been cancelled since the departure of Wyatt Mote on September 11, 2018.
